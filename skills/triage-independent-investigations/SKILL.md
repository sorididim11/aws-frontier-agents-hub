# Independent Investigation Policy

## Core Rule
Every incoming incident MUST be treated as an independent investigation.

## Do NOT link incidents even if:
- They involve the same service
- They have similar symptoms
- They occur within a short time window
- The same alarm triggers multiple times

## Action
When triaging a new incident:
1. Always choose Proceed (create new independent investigation)
2. Never choose Linked (do not correlate with existing investigations)
